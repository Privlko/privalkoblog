New Paper

2019/09/16

I wanted to include a small update on two recent publications. I know the blog is a bit neglected this is because I’ve started a postdoc and my first few reports are coming out. The first is about unpaid work in Ireland and its gender differences. I just caught the tail end of the work as I started, but I’m glad I had an input. Generally we find high levels of unpaid work in Ireland when compared to other countries in Europe.

The second is a peer reviewed paper from my PhD Thesis. In Quits and Ladders I compare the effects of quits and promotions on measures of job-fit and on monthly wages. This paper uses panel data from the UK which follows workers over an eight year period.
