Origin and Integration, a new report using Census 2016 data

2020/06/16

We have a new report!

We recently launched Origin and Integration, a piece of work that looks at migrant integration in Ireland. Here, we look at education, english language ability, unemployment and occupational attianment for migrants from over 100+ countries of birth. It’s a really exciting project, one that uses the entire migrant population in Ireland. You can read it here!
